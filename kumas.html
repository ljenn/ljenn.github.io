Kumas

Health Hack 2017.

Kumas Health Hack 2017 - Top 8 Finalist

  • Mar 2017
  • App

Kumas is a web & phone application that quantifies the qualitative data obtained from cognitive assessments performed during in-home patient monitoring. We focused the app toward patients with dementia or brain deterioration, and it strives to bridge the communication gap between patients and doctors by assisting a doctor's diagnosis of the severity of the condition.


Four wonderful friends of mine and I came together to bring this project to life during the 2017 hackathon hosted by Engineering World Health at UC San Diego.
