Kelly's Beauty Nail Spa

Website with booking system + New branding

Project Overview

About

Kelly's Beauty Nail Spa is a nail salon that offers nail, spa, facial, and waxing services to over 36,000 clients every year, averaging to 100 clients per day.

Role - UX Design Intern
Project Duration - Dec 2021 to Feb 2022
Team - Kelly Nguyen (CEO), Nhan To (Manager)
Responsibilities - Visual branding, Wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, design iterations, usability interviews, responsive design

Challenge

Online Booking System
Kelly’s Beauty Nail Spa is an established nail salon who needs to establish an online booking system in order to optimize the booking process and reduce wait times caused by overlapped bookings. Overlaps stresses nail technicians and disappoints clients who wait past their schedule time, which reduces client retention. Right now, the owner, manager, or receptionist in charge spends over 20% of working hours booking clients.

Visual Branding
Currently, the business has no logo. There is no consistent visual branding.

Goal

Kelly’s Beauty Nail Spa has a new website that integrates a friendly online appointment booking system, allowing new or returning customers to book appointments quickly, efficiently, and on-the-go. Our goal is to improve the flow and usability of the website by leveraging a hybrid system where clients can book appointments through 1) the website, 2) phone call, or 3) text message. Integrating an online appointment system would maximize efficiency for busy clients. It would also improve customer happiness by minimizing scheduling errors and free up time and energy for all employees.

User Research

Needs Assessment

  • Want flexibility and variety in scheduling an appointment - Users want the option to choose the nail technician who serves them, and they want to see available appointments in advance. (Note: Employees may go on vacation, so there needs to be a balance.)
  • Easy to change appointment - Simple, no hassle way to cancel or change appointments.
  • No hassle of logins - Many users found it tedious to create new accounts and have logins. It's time consuming and they don't want to log in to book an appointment.
  • Pay in person - Clients want to pay in person; the process to punch in their credit card information can be time consuming.
  • Don’t know who to choose - New customers aren’t sure which nail technician to choose. Many refer to Yelp reviews, but employees there may no longer be working at the salon.

    • Survey on Branding

  • Credibility comes from Yelp and Google reviews
  • Kelly is a core brand. The owner, Kelly, is a well known person among the area - she’s associated with friendliness, fun, and family-oriented values.
  • Quality, updated on the latest trends, easy process, and good service are important to customers.

    • Competitive Analysis

    Local nail salons offer:

  • Booking system through mobile app
  • Booking system through Yelp
  • Informative website

    • Areas we can stand out:

  • Easily accessible, simplified web interaction
  • Minimalistic user interface that’s not distracting or overloaded with information
  • Local salons don’t have a strong visual brand

    Testing / Design Iterations

    Usability Interview Findings

    Type: Unmoderated usability study
    Participants: 5 participants
    Location: United States, remote
    Length: 30 minutes

    High Priority

  • Website has 2 phone numbers - which to call?
  • Submissions for "get quote" is never checked
  • Hard to access system for booking appointments

    • Medium Priority

  • Employees may go on vacation, so clients can't book too far in advance; one week is an optimal balance
  • Gallery doesn’t showcase the full range of services and skills - there are three repeat pictures
  • Hours hard to find
  • Has 'Updates' section but never any updates

    • Low Priority

  • 'Job opportunities' page for hiring staff
  • Link to socials

    • Branding


    Focus - Cultural roots, immigrant background, family-oriented, loving, bold, elegance
    Lotus - A beautiful flower that rises from murky waters, often symbolizes overcoming adversities to flourish. Analogous to how Kelly, a first generation immigrant, built her nail salon from absolute scratch.
    9 petals - Petal per family - Kelly built her business to support 8 families plus her own. 9 is an auspicious number in Vietnamese culture.
    Red color - Represents love and the founder’s bold decisions. A symbol of luck in her culture.
    Story on website - Founder’s story on website makes the business more personable and relatable. It emphasizes all the values of the business.

    Mockups & High-fi Features

    Meet the team and updated services

    Listing out the team will encourage new clients to browse and make the best selection that is unique for themselves. Beauty is personal, and every journey is unique to the individual. It’s important for clients to have the option to choose the nail technician or esthetician of their preference.

    An updated services menu will tell customers exactly what services exist and their cost.


    Scheduling appointments online

    Clients can easily choose the service, the time, and see who's available. Instead of logging in with an account, they can verify by phone number to schedule an appointment and pay in person.


    Lookbook gallery

    Updated nails showcasing the variety of beauty trends, newest technology, and design capabilities that Kelly’s Beauty Nail Spa has to offer.
