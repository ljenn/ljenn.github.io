About

Anise Health is a culturally informed mental health and wellbeing platform that primarily targets the Asian American community. They are an early stage startup born from an accelerator program at Harvard Business School. I collaborated with an international team to build their marketing efforts from ground up.

Approach

Our brand palette consisted of soft pastels and earthy, neutral tones. Lines are intentionally more freeform, curvy, and less rigid or sharp - with hints of elements reminiscent of nature.
