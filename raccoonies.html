Raccoonies

  • Oct 2017
  • Biology
  • Research
  • Education
  • App

'Raccoonies' is a fun educational and interactive story that teaches a popular molecular biology technique called Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR). It uses fun, uniquely-developed characters and a crime-solving plot in an attempt to introduce the PCR process and its applications. It attempts to integrate cognitive pscyhology to enhance learning and memory retention.


This was built by an interdisciplinary team of four friends from three different departments: Biology, Computer Science, and Cognitive Science (one specializing in human-computer interaction, the other in machine learning).
