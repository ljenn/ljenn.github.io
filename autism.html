Autism Explained

Helping me and others better understand the diversity of autism.

Autism Explained

  • Oct - Dec 2017
  • Psychology
  • Education


'Autism Explained' is a project developed during my college Public Rhetoric course. It strives to build understanding of autistic people for those who are neurotypical.

I hope to tap into the heart of public rhetoric and use language that does not have negative connotations and that dehumanized autistic people. Rather, I want to explain the diversity of autism in a light that provides more understanding, empathy, and showcases them as just another human being like the rest of us with a different set of individual differences.

I acknowledge that it is sometimes offensive or frowned upon for a neurotypical person to explain autism when autistic people can speak for themselves. However, part of this project is also to help myself understand autism better and share my findings with others.
