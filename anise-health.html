Anise Health User Portal

The Challenge For their pilot program, Anise Health needed a user web portal that allows for a smooth onboarding process and user journey from registration to payment & scheduling.

Background

Anise Health is a culturally informed mental health telehealth platform for the Asian American community. They are an early stage startup founded by two Harvard Business graduates. Prior to this project, I created visual content as a part of their marketing team, growing their Instagram account from zero followers.

  • Role: Branding, UI + UX Designer
  • Duration: 8 weeks

Responsibilities

Given the strict time crunch, I rolled out weekly front-end specifications and designs for the engineers to implement. I had a strong understanding of Anise Health's branding expectations because I had worked on their marketing team for 6 months and had expanded their branding style.

Outcome

Anise Health launched a pilot product designed to be user and mobile friendly from the start of the client form to email formatting to their website portal.

User Research

Seven in-depth interviews were conducted to understand what the client would want in terms of usability and the user journey. Some of the insights answered include:

  • Do clients want more therapist options?
  • When in the user journey are clients comfortable sharing payment information?
  • What would encourage users to prioritize action items? Deadlines?
  • What items interrupt or inconvenience the flow of obtaining their immediate objective?

Approach

  • More peach and browns + less rosy tones to minimize projection of gender biases
  • Adhere to clean, clutter-free design to reduce unnecessary cognitive load
  • Rounded corners for a softer look and easier on the eyes

Design Priorities

I identified our top three priorities and focused on developing designs that struck a balance between them.

Functional & Friendly

Provides young Asian American professionals who are seeking therapy a convenient and functional platform to connect with the right therapist.

Fits the Anise Brand

Warm, earthy, and empowering - everything therapy should be. It needs to be inviting, given the long-standing stigma of mental health in Asian cultures and the resulting high percentages of Asians who need yet do not go to therapy.

Fast to Implement

Given our fast-approaching deadline, the designs needed to be quick to implement. My computer science background came in handy here.
