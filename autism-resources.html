List of helpful external resources.

  • Symptoms

  • More

  • What is Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)? [ Khan Academy link ]
      • A descriptive overview on the range and elements taken into account for ASD.

  • Analogy for Understanding Autism [ Autism Support Network link ]
      • An easy-to-understand analogy to better understand the big picture of ASD.

  • Autism Life Skills by Chantal Sicile-Kira
      • ISBN: 039953461X

  • What Are the Symptoms of Autism? [ Autism Speaks link ]
      • Lists an overview of the core characterizations of autism, as well as other traits that may (or may not) be closely or distantly correlated.

  • Does My Child Have Autism? [ HelpGuide.org link ]
      • Identifies common characterizations of autism in developing children.

  • Signs of Autism [ National Autism Association link ]
      • Identifies common signs that may signify autism.

  • Sensory Integration [ Autism Research Institute link ]
      • Provides a more technical understanding to how sensory processing works in any human being.

  • Sensory Processing in Autism: A Review of Neurophysiologic Findings (Research Publication) [ International Pediatric Research Foundation link ]
      • A science review on researching findings regarding sensory processing within ASD - a more challenging read.

  • Sensory Differences [ The National Autistic Society link ]
      • Provides a easy-to-understand introduction to sensory differences and provides examples of hyper- and hyposensitivities.

  • Autism - It's Different in Girls [ Scientific American link ]
      • Discusses different reasons why autism is different and more difficult to diagnose in females.
